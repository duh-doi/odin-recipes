Biryani Recipe

Description

The most mouth-watering rice dish this side of the Mississippi

Yeee Haaww!!

Ingredients

  • Cell Phone
  • Working Finger (left or right finger is fine, as long as it can press a screen multiple times)
  • One Indian Resturant's Phone Number/Address
  • Car (optional)

Steps

  1. Pick up your cell phone
  2. Using your working finger, type in the Indian Resturant's phone number into your cell phone
  3. When they pick up, say "Hi! Can I get a Chicken Biryani to go?"
  4. Answer any other related questions they may have
  5. Wait the designated time they told you to wait
  6. Using the address, go to the Indian Resturant and pick up your food
  7. Come back home and eat your delicious Biryani!
